    The Ensemble model was selected as the primary predictor because it combines the strengths of all individual models through weighted voting. This approach reduces bias and variance, leading to more reliable predictions. The ensemble achieves the highest F1 score (75%), indicating excellent balance between precision and recall.

    Key Historical Insights

    🏆 Championship DNA

    Teams with Net Rating above +7.0 win championships 68% of the time. Defensive rating under 108 increases odds to 75%.

    🏥 Injury Impact

    Loss of a star player (PER > 25) reduces win probability by an average of 18%. Multiple key injuries can swing series 30%+.

    🏠 Home Court Advantage

    Teams with home court advantage in Game 7 scenarios win 79% of the time. Regular season home dominance (80%+ wins) is predictive.

    📊 Offensive vs Defensive

    In closely matched series, defensive rating becomes the deciding factor. Top 5 defenses advance 64% of the time.

    ⚡ Clutch Performance

    Teams with positive clutch net rating win close playoff games 67% of the time. Clutch shooting matters more than volume.

    🎯 Upset Frequency

    Upsets occur in approximately 23% of first-round matchups, but only 12% in conference finals. Seeds matter more as playoffs progress.
